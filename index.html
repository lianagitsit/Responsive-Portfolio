About Me

I love building things. Solving problems. Tinkering. Creating. Getting lost in code and finding my way out.

My background is in community organizing, mobilizing people toward a common cause. I loved that work because it was fundamentally about getting people pumped to go out into the world and share what they cared about. As a developer, I can do that same thing thousands, even millions, of times over, creating applications that have the potential for a massive reach.
